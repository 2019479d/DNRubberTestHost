Natural Rubber(RSS3)

Ribbed smoked sheets(RSS3) is coagulated from high quality natural rubber latex. Rubber latex with chemicals processed into sheet, dried,smoked, and visually graded. RSS3 rubbers are used in the production of tyres, Tread carcass, inner tubes,Extruded hoses and foot wear etc.. The tyre industry is highest consumer of RSS3 rubber. The majority of rubber exported as RSS of which RSS 3 constitutes the major part.
